[![forthebadge](https://forthebadge.com/images/badges/made-with-python.svg)](https://forthebadge.com)[![forthebadge](https://forthebadge.com/images/badges/built-with-love.svg)](https://forthebadge.com)[![forthebadge](https://forthebadge.com/images/badges/powered-by-oxygen.svg)](https://forthebadge.com) [](https://github.com/Pycord-Development/pycord)[](https://www.python.org/downloads/release/python-382/) Nexus-Z Discord Bot =================== A Pokémon Statistics Discord bot for the use of calculating catch statistics for Max Raid Battles within Pokémon Sword and Shield ( /w all DLC). Also supports some functionality with Pokemon Legends Arceus. As well as supports various functions for Pokémon Go from Pokedex data to PVP calculations Discord Statistics & Top.gg --------------------------- [![Nexus-Z](https://top.gg/api/widget/674716932720558101.svg)](https://top.gg/bot/674716932720558101) Support and Progress -------------------- For all updates to the bot and other various information, the support server can be found here ----------> [Support Server](https://discord.gg/FqZ5KnT) Features -------- * Quick and easy access to the latest Pokémon Data, such as Base Stats / Abilities / Den Locations And More! * Accurately tuned catch rate calculator for Pokémon encountered in Max Raid Battles and (W.I.P) Wild grass encounters. * Efficient and fully detailed information for den look-up including Hidden Ability availability. * Various and useful Pokémon Go commands for Pokedex ( /w available moves ). Commands -------- `/catch` -------------------------------- Shows a detailed summary of catch rates for a given Pokémon & Pokeball in a Max Raid Den `/grass_catch` -------------------------------- Shows a detailed summary of catch rates for a given Pokémon & Pokeball (W.I.P) `/den` -------------------------------- Shows a list of Pokémon that belong to a den or Pokémon in which dens including their Non-HA/HAs `/pokedex` -------------------------------- Shows a detailed summary of a Pokémon’s latest Statistics (Gen 7 / Gen 8) `/ball` -------------------------------- Shows a summary of a Poké-ball’s statistics and animations `/natures` -------------------------------- Displays an in-depth Pokémon natures chart from Bulbapedia `/sprite` -------------------------------- Displays sprite of a Pokémon (Home Version) `/move` -------------------------------- Displays information about the move `/help` -------------------------------- Displays a help message with a table of commands Pokémon Go Commands ------------------- `/godex` -------------------------------- Dex Entry for the selected Pokémon `/gopvp` -------------------------------- Compares your Pokémon to the best of each league. `/gorocket` -------------------------------- Shows the current Team Go Rocket shadow Pokémon in rotation. `/gohundo` -------------------------------- Shows the CP of a hundred percent IV Pokémon. `/gopure` -------------------------------- Shows the example of before and after purifying a shadow Pokémon. `/gotohome` -------------------------------- Shows a rough estimation of Pokémon transfer from Go to Home Stat Translation. `/gosearchterms` -------------------------------- Learn what search terms you could use to help find Pokémon you need!
